Percy Jackson "P.J." Splagitowitz 1897-2024

"No, I don't know how I lived so long. Please stop asking." -Splagitowitz's Last Words. Percy Splagitowitz was a kind, caring husband and father of 27. He founded Healio at just 15 years old. Originally, the company made candlesticks. ”When I was little, I used to be too scared to sleep in the dark. My dear sweet mother, God rest her soul, would light a candle beside my bed to help me rest. It made the sounds of loose muskets and howling wolves outside a lot more bearable. I think that's why I wanted to make candles, at first. To spread that feeling of comfort. I wantws to make something that glows brighter than the sun, something that makes people feel as safe as the daylight.” -Percy Splagitowitz, 1919
