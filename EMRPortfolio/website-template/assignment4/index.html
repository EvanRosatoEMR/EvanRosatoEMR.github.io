A small tall tale.

Background: #140424, Text: #ffffff

.T

At the bottom of the mountain lay a tiny town filled with people. Many of the people in the tiny town wondered what was at the top of the mountain and tried to climb it, but none of them could make it all the way to the top. Then one day, seemingly out of nowhere, someone made it. They climbed atop the piles of frozen corpses that littered the mountain's paths, and stared down. The climber could no longer see the tiny town he once called home. When the climber returned to tell their story, nobody listened to them. Now that the mountain was stained with victory, nobody wanted to hear about it. The End.
